About Saad Syed

Saad Syed is an engineer from the Indianapolis area. He is new to web deelopment but is quickly developing coding skills in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. He has an extensive history as a pharmacy technician. His background in the pharmaceutical field has led to experiance in handling multiple tasks at once such as filling 200 scripts a day, giving and consulting patients on vaccines, and administering covid tests daily.

Saad is passionate about each project he works on no matter the size. He defines himself as a "forever student," ready to build on his past foundations in pharmacy, and start a new path in web development through continued courses and professional training.

Saad's current and past experiances have led him to excel in projects both completed and in progress. Saad has partnered with many different groups to help lead successful endeavors, in the form of web building, as well as vaccination clinics to improve public health.

Saad is currently still employed in the pharmaceutical industry, but is looking for a career change once he has a few more web projects under his belt.
